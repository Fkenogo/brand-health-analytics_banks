## Part 4: Technical Implementation Considerations

### For a No-Code Developer:

**Recommended Platforms:**
1. **Tableau** - Best for complex visualizations, handles large datasets
2. **Power BI** - Microsoft ecosystem, good for sharing with business teams
3. **Google Data Studio** (Looker Studio) - Free, web-based, easy to share
4. **Metabase** - Open source, good for SQL queries
5. **Retool** - Best for custom dashboards with interactivity

**Data Pipeline:**
1. Survey data (likely in CSV or Google Sheets)
2. Data cleaning/transformation layer (can use Google Sheets formulas or Airtable)
3. Dashboard connection (direct connection or scheduled refresh)

**Key Calculations Needed:**

1. **Awareness Quality** = (Top-of-Mind ÷ Total Awareness) × 100
2. **Conversion Rate** = (Ever Used ÷ Aware) × 100
3. **Retention Rate** = (Currently Using ÷ Ever Used) × 100
4. **Preference Rate** = (Preferred Bank ÷ Currently Using) × 100
5. **Momentum Score** = Weighted average of (Awareness Growth + Conversion + Retention + Adoption)
6. **NPS** = % Promoters (9-10) - % Detractors (0-6)
7. **Loyalty Index** = Weighted sum of segment distributions

## Part 5: Dashboard Design Best Practices

### Color Palette Recommendations:
- **Primary Brand Color** (e.g., Blue for BK) - Use for your focal bank
- **Secondary Colors** - Consistent colors for each competitor
- **Status Colors:**
  - Green: Positive metrics, growth, promoters
  - Red: Negative metrics, decline, detractors  
  - Yellow/Orange: Neutral, passives, warnings
  - Purple: Consideration stage
  - Gray: Inactive or N/A

### Layout Principles:
1. **F-Pattern Reading** - Most important metrics in top-left
2. **Progressive Disclosure** - Summary cards first, detailed charts below
3. **Whitespace** - Your current designs are good but ensure breathing room
4. **Responsive Design** - Must work on desktop and tablet

### Interactivity Features:
1. **Filters:**
   - Date range selector
   - Country selector
   - Bank selector
   - Demographic filters (age, gender, etc.)
   
2. **Drill-Downs:**
   - Click on any metric to see detailed breakdown
   - Click on bank name to see full profile
   
3. **Tooltips:**
   - Hover over any chart for more details
   - Metric definitions on hover
   
4. **Exportability:**
   - Export to PDF for presentations
   - Export data to CSV for further analysis

## Part 7: Key Metrics Glossary (For Your Technical Team)

| Metric | Definition | Calculation | Data Source |
|--------|-----------|-------------|-------------|
| **Total Awareness** | % who know the bank | (Respondents aware ÷ Total sample) × 100 | Section C, Q3 |

| **Ever Used** | % who have tried bank | (Ever Used ÷ Total sample) × 100 | Section D, Q1 |
| **Current Usage** | % actively using | (Current Users ÷ Total sample) × 100 | Section D, Q2 |
| **Preferred Bank (BUMO)** | Primary bank | (Preferred ÷ Current Users) × 100 | Section D, Q3 |
| **Trial Rate** | Conversion to trial | (Ever Used ÷ Aware) × 100 | Derived |
| **Retention Rate** | Staying active | (Current ÷ Ever Used) × 100 | Derived |
| **Preference Rate** | Becoming primary | (Preferred ÷ Current) × 100 | Derived |
| **NPS** | Net Promoter Score | %Promoters - %Detractors | Section E, Q5 |
| **Future Intent** | Likelihood to use | Avg score 0-10 | Section D, Q1 |
| **Relevance** | Suitable for me | % who select bank | Section D, Q2 |

## Part 9: Additional Questionnaire Enhancements

To enable more powerful analytics, consider adding these questions in future survey versions:

### Behavioral Questions:
1. **Primary Banking Needs:** "What do you primarily use banking services for?" (Savings, Loans, Transfers, Investments, etc.)
2. **Digital vs Physical:** "How do you mainly access banking?" (Branch, ATM, Mobile App, Website, USSD)
3. **Satisfaction Drivers:** "Rate your satisfaction with [Bank] on:" (Fees, Customer Service, Digital Experience, Branch Access, etc.)

### Attitudinal Questions:
4. **Brand Attributes:** "Which words describe [Bank]?" (Trustworthy, Modern, Affordable, etc.)
5. **Switching Likelihood:** "How likely are you to switch your primary bank in the next 6 months?"
6. **Reason for Choice:** "Why did you choose [Bank] as your preferred bank?"

### Contextual Questions:
7. **Source of Awareness:** "How did you first hear about [Bank]?" (TV, Social Media, Friend, Branch, etc.)
8. **Account Opening Recency:** "When did you open your account?" (This month, This year, 1-2 years ago, 3+ years ago)
